1.What is good screen-based design?

Good screen based design is when the audience/user has an easy experience navigating the material. It is also visually appealing for on-screen experience, where the design is helping rather than distracting.

2.How is art-making online different then art-making offline?

Online is more technical because it can reach a broader audience within a short amount of time whereas offline art is made for the user to run into in a public setting.

3.What do you think is the purpose of the web?

The purpose of the web is to share information and give access to people all over the world. The web is meant to give people access to all of the information in the world as up to date as possible.

4.How has your view of the internet / how you experience being online changed over time?

My view of the internet has changed overtime in the sense that it originally started as a place for communication and has turned into so much more. I see it as a place of opportunity, for jobs and a future for myself. I see that my experience being online has changed from when I was little, going on the computer to play games to learning everything at the tips of my fingers.

5.How does the web impact graphic design?

The web impacts graphic design by making it more reachable to a wider audience. Graphic design is visual communication, but having it on the web not only makes it communicative to a wider audience but also interactive for everyone. The user is able to interact with this form of art from their devices.
